About StudyFlow

StudyFlow was created as a capstone project to address a common problem faced by students: the lack of a simple, clean, and intuitive tool to organize their studies. In a world full of complex and feature-heavy applications, StudyFlow aims to provide a lightweight and straightforward solution.
